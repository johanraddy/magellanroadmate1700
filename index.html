How to Update Magellan RoadMate 1700?

  1. 🔌 Connect your GPS device to your computer via USB cable.
  2. 💾 Download and install Content Manager from Magellan’s official website.
  3. 🖥️ Launch the application and log in using your Magellan account.
  4. 📥 Click on “Check for Updates.”
  5. 📊 Download available firmware and map updates.
  6. ✅ Once complete, safely disconnect and restart your GPS.
